# The AI Startup Game

An interactive business simulation game where you navigate the challenges of building an AI startup in the UK. Make strategic decisions across research, product development, sales, and people management as you race to hit milestones before running out of runway.

## ğŸ“– About

The AI Startup Game is an educational simulation that puts you in the role of a founder building an AI company. You'll face realistic challenges including:

- **Quarterly decision-making** across four domains: Science, Development, Marketing, and HR
- **Resource management** - balance cash, burn rate, and equity
- **Milestone pressure** - hit your targets before deadlines or face the board
- **Market dynamics** - navigate changing conditions, rivals, and unexpected shocks
- **Team building** - hire specialists and manage team morale
- **Fundraising** - pitch to investors and negotiate terms

## ğŸ¯ How to Play

1. **Choose your venture type** - Select from different AI startup archetypes (BioTech, Industrial, FinTech, or Creative)
2. **Name your company** and customize your founding team
3. **Make quarterly decisions** - Allocate action points to research, development, sales, or people initiatives
4. **Hit milestones** - Complete three major milestones to win
5. **Manage your runway** - Don't run out of cash!
6. **Choose your ending** - If you succeed, decide your company's ultimate path
